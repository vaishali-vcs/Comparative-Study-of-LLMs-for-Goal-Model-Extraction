- 'Actors': 
  - Kids and Youth
  - Counseling Service

- 'SoftGoals': 
  - Anonymity: The system should ensure the privacy and confidentiality of the users.
  - Convenience: The system should be easy to use and accessible from anywhere.
  - Promptness: The system should provide immediate response and quick service.
  - Effectiveness: The counseling service should be able to effectively address the concerns and issues of the users.
  
- 'Goals': 
  - To provide counseling service to Kids and Youth.
  - To offer different modes of communication for counseling (text messaging, Cyber Café, portal, Chat Rooms).
  
- 'Tasks': 
  - Develop a system that offers counseling service.
  - Implement features for text messaging, Cyber Café, portal, and Chat Rooms.
  - Ensure the system is user-friendly and easy to navigate.
  - Train counselors to provide effective counseling.
  
- 'Resources': 
  - Trained Counselors
  - System Developers
  - IT Infrastructure (servers, databases, etc.)
  - Communication Channels (text messaging, Cyber Café, portal, Chat Rooms)
  
- 'Contribution links': 
  - The trained counselors contribute to the effectiveness of the counseling service.
  - The system developers contribute to the development and maintenance of the system.
  
- 'Dependencies links': 
  - The counseling service depends on the IT infrastructure and the trained counselors.
  
- 'Decomposition links':
  - The goal of providing counseling service is decomposed into tasks like system development, feature implementation, and counselor training.
