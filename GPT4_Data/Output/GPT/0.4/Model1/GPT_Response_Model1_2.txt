- 'Actors': 
  - Traffic Simulator: This system is the main actor that enables traffic flow simulation and aids in the development of efficient road traffic engineering and design.

- 'SoftGoals': 
  - Efficient Road Traffic Engineering: This is a softgoal as there's no clear, objective measure of satisfaction. It involves improving the functionality of the traffic system.
  - Clear Visualizations: This is another softgoal as it aims to improve the user interface and user experience of the system.
  - Highlighting Congestion: This softgoal is related to the system's capability to identify and highlight areas of traffic congestion.
  - Demonstrating Traffic Flow and Bottlenecks: This softgoal is related to the system's ability to animate and demonstrate the flow of traffic and identify bottlenecks.

- 'Goals': 
  - Traffic Flow Simulation: The main goal of the Traffic Simulator is to simulate the flow of traffic.

- 'Tasks': 
  - Develop Traffic Flow Simulation: This task involves developing the capability to simulate traffic flow.
  - Design Density Maps: This task involves creating density maps to highlight congestion.
  - Create Animations: This task involves creating animations to demonstrate traffic flow and bottlenecks.

- 'Resources': 
  - Traffic Data: This resource is required for the Traffic Simulator to perform its tasks.
  - Software Tools: These resources are needed to develop the traffic flow simulation, design density maps, and create animations.

- 'Contribution links': 
  - Traffic Flow Simulation contributes positively to Efficient Road Traffic Engineering.
  - Clear Visualizations contribute positively to Efficient Road Traffic Engineering.
  - Highlighting Congestion and Demonstrating Traffic Flow and Bottlenecks contribute positively to Clear Visualizations.

- 'Dependencies links': 
  - The tasks of Developing Traffic Flow Simulation, Designing Density Maps, and Creating Animations are dependent on the availability of Traffic Data and Software Tools.

- 'Decomposition links': 
  - The goal of Traffic Flow Simulation is decomposed into the tasks of Developing Traffic Flow Simulation, Designing Density Maps, and Creating Animations.
