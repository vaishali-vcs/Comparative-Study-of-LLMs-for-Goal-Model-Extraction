- 'Actors': 
  - Counselors: They are the stakeholders who offer online counseling services to children. 

- 'SoftGoals': 
  - Job Satisfaction: Counselors should strive to prevent burnout in order to attain job satisfaction.
  - Prevent Burnout: Counselors should strive to prevent burnout while offering their services.
  - Security: The online counseling services should be secure to protect the children's information.
  - Reliability: The online counseling services should be reliable so that they are always available when needed.
  - Speed: The online counseling services should be fast enough to provide immediate assistance.

- 'Goals': 
  - Assist as many children as they can: This is the primary goal of the counselors.
  - Actively listen to the children's needs: This is another goal of the counselors.

- 'Tasks': 
  - Offer online counseling services: This is the task that the counselors need to perform to achieve their goals.
  - Use cyber cafes and portal chat rooms: These are the platforms that the counselors can use to offer their services.

- 'Resources': 
  - Cyber cafes: These are the resources that the counselors can use to offer their services.
  - Portal chat rooms: These are also resources that the counselors can use to offer their services.

- 'Contribution links': 
  - Offering online counseling services contributes positively to assisting as many children as they can.
  - Actively listening to the children's needs contributes positively to assisting as many children as they can.
  - Preventing burnout contributes positively to job satisfaction.

- 'Dependencies links': 
  - The task of offering online counseling services depends on the resources of cyber cafes and portal chat rooms.

- 'Decomposition links':
  - The goal of assisting as many children as they can is decomposed into the tasks of offering online counseling services and actively listening to their needs.
