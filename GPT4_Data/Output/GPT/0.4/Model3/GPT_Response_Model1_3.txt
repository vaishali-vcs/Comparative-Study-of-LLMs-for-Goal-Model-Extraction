'Actors': 
- User

'SoftGoals': 
- User-friendly interface 
- Reliable information about recycling centers 
- Secure access to the system 
- Fast loading of map and information 
- High data integrity

'Goals': 
- View all locations of recycling centers on a map
- Get the hours of each recycling facility

'Tasks': 
- Implement a feature to display recycling centers on a map 
- Implement a feature to provide information about the hours of each recycling facility

'Resources': 
- Database of recycling centers and their operating hours 
- Map service to display locations

'Contribution links': 
- Displaying recycling centers on a map contributes positively to the goal of viewing all locations 
- Providing information about the hours of each facility contributes positively to the goal of arranging drop-offs

'Dependencies links': 
- The task of displaying recycling centers on a map depends on the availability of a map service and a database of recycling centers
- The task of providing information about the hours of each facility depends on the availability of a database with this information

'Decomposition links': 
- The goal of viewing all locations of recycling centers on a map can be decomposed into the task of implementing a feature to display these locations 
- The goal of getting the hours of each recycling facility can be decomposed into the task of implementing a feature to provide this information
