Here is the list of intentional elements extracted from the user story:

- 'Actors': 
    - Kids and Youth
    - Counseling Service Provider

- 'SoftGoals': 
    - Anonymity: The service should prioritize anonymity to ensure the privacy of the users.
    - Convenience: The service should be user-friendly and easy to access.
    - Promptness: The service should offer immediate response to the users.
    - Effectiveness: The service should be able to address the issues of the users effectively.
    - Security: The service should ensure the safety of the users' information.
    - Reliability: The service should be dependable and consistent in its performance.
    - Data Integrity: The service should ensure that the users' information is accurate and consistent.

- 'Goals': 
    - To provide a counseling service that prioritizes anonymity, convenience, promptness, and effectiveness.

- 'Tasks': 
    - Utilize text messaging for counseling.
    - Opt for Cyber Café for counseling.
    - Visit the portal for counseling.
    - Use Chat Rooms for counseling.

- 'Resources': 
    - Text Messaging platform
    - Cyber Café
    - Counseling Portal
    - Chat Rooms

- 'Contribution links': 
    - Text messaging, Cyber Café, the portal, and Chat Rooms contribute to the convenience of the service.
    - Anonymity contributes to the security and reliability of the service.

- 'Dependencies links': 
    - The effectiveness of the counseling service depends on the availability of the resources (Text Messaging platform, Cyber Café, Counseling Portal, Chat Rooms).

- 'Decomposition links':
    - The goal of providing a counseling service is decomposed into tasks of utilizing different platforms (Text Messaging, Cyber Café, Counseling Portal, Chat Rooms) for counseling.
