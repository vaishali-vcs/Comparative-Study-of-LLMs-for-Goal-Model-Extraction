'Actors':

'SoftGoals': 
- Anonymity: The counseling service should ensure the privacy and confidentiality of the kids and youth.
- Convenience: The counseling service should be easily accessible and user-friendly.
- Promptness: The counseling service should provide immediate response and support.
- Effectiveness: The counseling service should be able to address the issues and concerns of the kids and youth effectively.

'Goals': 
- To provide a counseling service that prioritizes anonymity.
- To offer a convenient, prompt, and effective counseling service.

'Tasks': 
- Develop a texting messaging option for counseling.
- Establish a Cyber Café for counseling.
- Develop a portal for counseling.
- Establish chat rooms for counseling.

'Resources':
- Counseling service professionals.
- Technology infrastructure for text messaging, Cyber Café, portal, and chat rooms.

'Contribution links': 
- The development of various counseling options (text messaging, Cyber Café, portal, chat rooms) contributes to the goal of providing a convenient counseling service.
- The prioritization of anonymity contributes to the softgoal of ensuring privacy and confidentiality.

'Dependency links': 
- The effectiveness of the counseling service depends on the skills and expertise of the counseling service professionals.
- The promptness of the counseling service depends on the technology infrastructure.
