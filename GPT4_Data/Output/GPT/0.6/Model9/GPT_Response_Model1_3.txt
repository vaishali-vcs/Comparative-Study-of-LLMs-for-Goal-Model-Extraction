- 'Actors': 
    - Counselors: They offer online counseling services to children and also strive to prevent burnout in order to attain job satisfaction.

- 'SoftGoals': 
    - Job Satisfaction: This is a non-functional requirement. Counselors should achieve job satisfaction by balancing their workload and preventing burnout.
    - Prevent Burnout: This is also a non-functional requirement. Counselors should prevent burnout by managing their workload and maintaining a healthy work-life balance.

- 'Goals': 
    - Assist as many children as they can: This is a functional requirement. Counselors should be able to assist as many children as they can through their online counseling services.
    - Actively listen to children's needs: This is also a functional requirement. Counselors should be able to actively listen to and understand the needs of the children they are assisting.

- 'Tasks': 
    - Offer online counseling services: This is the main task that counselors need to perform in order to achieve their goals and softgoals. This includes providing services through cyber cafes and portal chat rooms.

- 'Resources': 
    - Cyber Cafes: This is a resource that counselors can use to offer their online counseling services.
    - Portal Chat Rooms: This is another resource that counselors can use to offer their online counseling services.

- 'Contribution links': 
    - Offering online counseling services contributes positively to the goal of assisting as many children as possible.
    - Actively listening to children's needs contributes positively to the goal of assisting as many children as possible.
    - Preventing burnout contributes positively to the softgoal of achieving job satisfaction.

- 'Dependencies links': 
    - The task of offering online counseling services is dependent on the availability of the resources, i.e., cyber cafes and portal chat rooms.
    - The goal of assisting as many children as possible is dependent on the task of offering online counseling services and actively listening to children's needs.

- 'Decomposition links':
    - The goal of assisting as many children as possible can be decomposed into the tasks of offering online counseling services and actively listening to children's needs.
    - The softgoal of achieving job satisfaction can be decomposed into the task of preventing burnout.
