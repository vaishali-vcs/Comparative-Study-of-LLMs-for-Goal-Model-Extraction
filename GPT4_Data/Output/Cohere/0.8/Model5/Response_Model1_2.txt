## Intentional elements of GRL:
- **Actors**: Represent stakeholders of the system or the system itself. In GRL, actors are represented as circles with their names inside. 
- **Softgoals**: Represent the intentions of the actors or the conditions that need to be fulfilled. A softgoal might be a non-functional requirement such as performance, security or reliability.
