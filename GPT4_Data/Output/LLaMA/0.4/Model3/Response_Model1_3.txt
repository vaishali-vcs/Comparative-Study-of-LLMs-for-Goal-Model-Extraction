Actors: 

* User (representing a stakeholder of the system or the system itself)

SoftGoals:

* To provide the user with a convenient and accessible way to find recycling centers and their hours of operation.
* To enable the user to plan their route to drop off waste effectively and efficiently.
* To improve the user experience by providing a map view and detailed information about the recycling centers.

Goals:

* To display all locations of recycling centers on a map.
* To provide the user with the hours of each recycling facility.

Tasks:

* To create a map view of all recycling centers.
* To retrieve and display the hours of each recycling facility.

Resources:

* A database of recycling centers and their hours of operation.
* A mapping API to display the locations of the recycling centers on a map.

Contribution Links:

* The map view of all recycling centers will contribute to the user's ability to plan their route to drop off waste.
* The hours of each recycling facility will contribute to the user's ability to arrange drop-offs on their off days or during after-work hours.

Dependencies Links:

* The map view of all recycling centers will depend on the availability of a database of recycling centers and their hours of operation.
* The hours of each recycling facility will depend on the availability of the mapping API to display the locations of the recycling centers on a map.

Decomposition Links:

* The task of creating a map view of all recycling centers can be decomposed into sub-tasks such as retrieving the locations of the recycling centers and displaying them on a map.
* The task of retrieving the hours of each recycling facility can be decomposed into sub-tasks such as retrieving the hours from a database or API and formatting them for display on the map.
