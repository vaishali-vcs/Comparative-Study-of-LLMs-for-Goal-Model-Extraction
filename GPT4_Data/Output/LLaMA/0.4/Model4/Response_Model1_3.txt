Actors:

* Executive
* HR Employee
* Employee

SoftGoals:

* Full access to data related to the company for the executive
* Modify data of the company for the HR employee
* View data of the company for the employee and HR employee

Goals:

* Achieve full access to data for the executive
* Modify data of the company for the HR employee
* View data of the company for the employee and HR employee

Tasks:

* Provide the executive with full access to data related to the company
* Allow the HR employee to modify the data of the company
* Display data of the company for the employee and HR employee

Resources:

* Data related to the company
* Access to data related to the company

Contribution Links:

* The executive's access to data related to the company contributes to the goal of achieving full access to data
* The HR employee's ability to modify the data of the company contributes to the goal of modifying data
* The employee's ability to view data of the company contributes to the goal of viewing data

Dependency Links:

* The executive's access to data related to the company depends on the availability of data
* The HR employee's ability to modify the data of the company depends on the availability of data and the executive's access to data
* The employee's ability to view data of the company depends on the availability of data and the executive's access to data

Decomposition Links:

* The goal of achieving full access to data can be decomposed into sub-goals of providing the executive with full access to data and allowing the HR employee to modify the data of the company
* The goal of modifying data can be decomposed into sub-goals of allowing the HR employee to modify the data of the company and providing the employee with access to data
* The goal of viewing data can be decomposed into sub-goals of displaying data of the company for the employee and HR employee

Note: The above list of intentional elements is not exhaustive and can be further expanded as per the requirements of the system.
