Actors: 

* Kids and Youth (as stakeholders of the system)

SoftGoals: 

* Anonymity: The system should provide anonymity to the users, ensuring that their personal information is not shared or disclosed without their consent.
	+ Specifications:
		- The system should use end-to-end encryption for all communication channels (text messaging, Cyber Café, portal, Chat Rooms).
		- The system should provide a pseudonym or avatar for users to maintain their anonymity.
		- The system should not collect or store any personal information (e.g., name, address, phone number) from users.
		- The system should allow users to delete their account and all associated information upon request.
* Convenience: The system should provide convenient and accessible counseling services to users.
	+ Specifications:
		- The system should offer multiple communication channels (text messaging, Cyber Café, portal, Chat Rooms) to accommodate users' preferences.
		- The system should provide a user-friendly interface and intuitive navigation for all communication channels.
		- The system should offer a 24/7 availability for counseling services.
		- The system should provide a quick response time for messages and queries.

Goals:

* To provide effective counseling services to Kids and Youth.

Tasks:

* To develop a user-friendly and intuitive interface for the system.
* To implement end-to-end encryption for all communication channels.
* To create a pseudonym or avatar for users to maintain their anonymity.
* To provide 24/7 availability for counseling services.
* To ensure quick response times for messages and queries.
