Actors:

* Kids and Youth (stakeholders)

SoftGoals:

* Anonymity: Kids and Youth should be able to seek counseling services without revealing their identities.
	+ Specifications:
		- The system should provide a secure and anonymous communication channel for text messaging, Cyber Café, portal, and Chat Rooms.
		- The system should not retain any personal information or data that could be used to identify the user.
		- The system should provide a feature to allow users to create a pseudonym or alias to protect their identity.
* Convenience: Kids and Youth should be able to access counseling services at their convenience.
	+ Specifications:
		- The system should provide a user-friendly interface that is accessible through various channels (text messaging, Cyber Café, portal, and Chat Rooms).
		- The system should be available 24/7, with no time restrictions.
		- The system should provide a feature to allow users to schedule appointments or sessions.
* Promptness: Kids and Youth should be able to receive prompt and effective counseling services.
	+ Specifications:
		- The system should provide a response time of less than 24 hours for text messaging, Cyber Café, portal, and Chat Rooms.
		- The system should provide a feature to allow users to request immediate assistance.
		- The system should provide a feature to allow users to rate the effectiveness of the counseling services.
