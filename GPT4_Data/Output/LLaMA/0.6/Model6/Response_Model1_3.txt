Actors:

* The wireless telephony company as the system's stakeholder
* The system's users who want high performance and low costs

SoftGoals:

* High performance: The system should be capable of handling a large number of users and providing high-quality service
* Low costs: The system should minimize infrastructure alterations and optimize hardware utilization to keep costs low

Specifications:

* The system should be able to handle a minimum of 10,000 concurrent users
* The system should be able to provide a throughput of at least 100 Mbps
* The system should be able to support a variety of wireless communication standards
* The system should be able to integrate with existing infrastructure without significant modifications

Goals:

* Achieve high throughput
* Minimize infrastructure alterations

Tasks:

* Optimize hardware utilization
* Implement advanced signal processing algorithms
* Use efficient communication protocols

Resources:

* High-performance hardware (e.g., servers, routers, switches)
* Advanced signal processing software
* Efficient communication protocols

Contribution Links:

* The system's high throughput will contribute to the satisfaction of the soft goal of high performance
* The system's low costs will contribute to the satisfaction of the soft goal of low costs

Dependencies Links:

* The system's performance is dependent on the availability of high-performance hardware and advanced signal processing algorithms
* The system's low costs are dependent on the ability to integrate with existing infrastructure without significant modifications
